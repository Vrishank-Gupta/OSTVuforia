The ExploreWorld app has been developed as Semester project by Vrishank Gupta at Netaji Subhas University Of Technology, Dwarka.

Key Functionality

  • Play video on targets for devices that support (devices that don't support will play full screen).
  • Optionally play video full screen in native player.

Targets

Targets are stored on the Vuforia cloud web service and contain video url as a metadata package.

Instructions

  • Point camera at target to view
  • Tap video to play
  • Double tap to access options menu
